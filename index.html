    About Us

    Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre (AIMSRC)

    Established in 2014 and affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, AIMSRC aims to develop expert medical professionals and serve society by giving capable doctors who have a solid, 360-degree understanding of medicine and healthcare. The Students are trained by way of challenging academics and a well-balanced extracurricular program. The Students also get hands-on experience in well-equipped OTs as well as rural and urban field practice drives. It provides excellent academic infrastructure and world-class facilities for the overall academic growth and development of the students. It also facilitates a value-centric cultural approach to achieve global academic excellence.

    Eligibility Criteria

    Under-Graduate -

    MBBS/B.Sc Nursing/BPT/Allied Health

      Students must be 17 years old by December 31st of the admission year.

      Students must secure a minimum of 50% in PCB. In the case of SC/ST students, 40 % and 45% for OBC.

      For MBBS, Appearing in the National Eligibility and Entrance Examination (NEET-UG) is mandatory. (Government, Deemed & Private Colleges).

      For B.Sc Nursing, Appearing in NEET-UG is not mandatory for Private and Deemed Colleges. If the student is looking for Govt. For colleges, NEET-UG is mandatory.

      For BPT, Appearing in NEET-UG is not mandatory for Private and Deemed Colleges. If the student is looking for Govt. For colleges, NEET-UG is mandatory.

      For Allied Health, Appearing in NEET-UG is not mandatory for Private and Deemed Colleges. If the student is looking for Govt. For colleges, NEET-UG is mandatory.

    Post-Graduate -

    MD/MS

      Students seeking admission must have earned an MBBS from a recognized university.

      For MD/MS, Appearing in the National Eligibility and Entrance Examination (NEET-PG) is mandatory.

      Students must have completed a 1-year internship.

    Campus Life

    AIMSRC has a lush green campus of 30-40 acres equipped with high-tech infrastructure and innovative facilities.

    • Hostel Facilities:

      AIMSRC has high-quality modern hostels, with 24/7 security.

    • Central Library :

      The Central Library has more than 10,000+ collections of medical books in pre-clinical/para-clinical.E-Library and E-Journals facilitate higher learning and research.

    • Auditorium:

      With a seating capacity of 2000 people with air-conditioning, the auditorium serves Club activities, Cultural events, Conferences, and Seminars.

    • Recreational & Cultural Activities:

      AIMSRC has facilities for outdoor games including Athletics, Football, Cricket fields, Basketball, Volleyball, Tennis, and Badminton courts.

    • Transport:

      Shuttle Services are available from campus to Devanahalli. Ambulance with Monitors, Defibrillators, Ventilators, and continuous Oxygen Supply is always kept ready to serve emergencies.
